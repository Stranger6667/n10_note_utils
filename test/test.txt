* **Noncouscious processes**(**非意识过程**): not typically available to consciousness or memory
  
  * for example: the regulation of blood pressure(血压调节)， your nervous system monitors physiological information to detect and act on changes continually, without your awareness
  * At certain times some ordinarily noncouscious activities can be made conscious: for example, you can choose to exercise conscious control over your pattern of breathing
  
* **Preconscious memory**(**前意识记忆**): Memory that is not currently conscious but that can **ealily** be called into consciousness when necessary. (例如：你在想你上一个假期中的经历，或者有关《哈姆雷特》的作者)
  
  * The **preconscious** refers to the thoughts you aren't actively thinking of but can call to mind **easily** given the right trigger （一个常驻内存的后台进程，当有需要的时候，**很容易**就能切换到前台来)
  * 与preconscious相应的两个概念：conscious, unconscious
    * conscious: those we're aware of **right now**，(也就是前台进程)
    * unconscious: repressed to the point that you can't remember them without extreme 
      effort and specialized help. (没有进程，需要开一个新进程来进行处理)
* **Unattended information**(**未被注意的信息**): attention只能focus当前知觉信息的一个子集，其他的称为unattended information
* **Unconscious**(无意识): 当你无法用你的意识来解释你的某些行为时，你才会认识到无意识信息的存在
